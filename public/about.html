About OAP

Operations • Analytics • Performance

OAP is a secure, internal-first operations platform built to support real operational environments where accuracy, accountability, and speed matter.

What is OAP?

OAP (Operations • Analytics • Performance) is a web-based platform designed to centralize operational records, performance metrics, and analytical insights into a single trusted system. It is purpose-built for complex environments such as airport ground services, where operations span multiple teams, shifts, and responsibilities.

Who is it for?

  • Operational teams managing daily activities
  • Supervisors tracking incidents, delays, and performance
  • Managers reviewing trends, accountability, and outcomes
  • Organizations requiring structured, auditable operational data

Why OAP exists

Many operational environments rely on fragmented spreadsheets, emails, and manual reports. OAP was created to eliminate this fragmentation by providing a single source of truth, enabling teams to move from reactive reporting to proactive decision-making.

Trust & Architecture

  • Frontend hosted on Cloudflare Pages
  • Secure access control via Cloudflare routing and functions
  • Backend services separated from the user interface
  • Data protected using role-based access and row-level security

This architecture ensures scalability, security, and operational integrity.

Who is behind OAP?

OAP is designed and built by kas, with direct experience in operational environments and a focus on building systems that reflect real-world workflows rather than generic software assumptions.
